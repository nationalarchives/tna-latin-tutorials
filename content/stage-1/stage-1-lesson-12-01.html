Adverbs

An adverb describes a verb. It provides information about how the verb is carried out. Adverbs usually come before the verb. They do not decline. Concentrate on learning words marked with an asterisk* first.

You have already used some adverbs:

item likewise
non not

Here are some other common adverbs

celeriter quickly
falso falsely
libere freely*
quiete peacefully*

Did you notice that English adverbs often end in ‘-ly’? However, not all do
